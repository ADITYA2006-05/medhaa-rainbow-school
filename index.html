INTEGRATED CBSE Curriculum

Medhaa's Rainbow School follows an Integrated CBSE Curriculum that blends core academics with life skills, co-curricular activities, and digital learning — nurturing well-rounded students ready for tomorrow's world.

📖

Languages

English, Hindi & Telugu with strong emphasis on communication, reading, and writing skills.

🔢

Mathematics

Conceptual and application-based Maths as per CBSE standards from foundational to advanced levels.

🔬

Science

Hands-on experiments and lab activities promoting scientific inquiry and critical thinking.

🌍

Social Studies

History, Geography, Civics and Economics integrated to build informed and empathetic citizens.

💻
